## Overview

Celestial is an online jewelry marketplace that connects customers with skilled merchants. We offer a seamless shopping experience for buying and selling exquisite jewelry pieces, from rings and earrings to necklaces and custom collections like "Cosmic Elegance".

## Features

-   **Product Browsing**: Explore various categories including Rings, Earrings, Necklaces.
-   **Shopping Cart**: Add items to cart and manage your orders.
-   **User Accounts**: Sign up, login, and manage your profile.
-   **Merchant Platform**: Secure platform for merchants to showcase their designs.
